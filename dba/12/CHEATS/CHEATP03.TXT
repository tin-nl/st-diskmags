the intended "ZICKE ZACKE HšHNERKACKE".  In the game,  keys 1-7 take you to the 
corresponding levels, F1-F5 select weapons, "G" gives you gold. Other functions 
keys also do bits.

 James Pond - Millenium

 Type "MR2" and press return. You can now toggle cheat mode by pressing RETURN; 
all  inter-level locks will disappear by pressing "D".  You can jump to  levels 
(once having activated the cheat) by entering "Z-1",  "X-2",  "C-3", "V-4", "B-
5",  "N-6",  "M-7",  ",-8", ".-9" or "/-10" (the keys on the lowest row of a UK
