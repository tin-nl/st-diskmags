 Pitfighter

 Type in "LOBSTERS" while playing, followed by "1" to "0" for the levels. Press 
"C" to go to championship level, "L" for the elimination match.

 Platoon - Ocean

 Type in "HAMBURGER HILL" on the title screen.  During the game,  F2 will  take 
you to the explosive,  F3 to the bridge,  F4 to the village and F5 will get you 
immunity.

 Police Quest - Sierra on Line

 Pressing [ALTERNATE] and "D" will get you into special mode.  You can use "TP" 
to  jump to any room,  and "GET ALL" followed by an object number to  get  that 
object.

 Police Quest II - Sierra on Line

 Pressing [ALTERNATE] and "D" will get you into special mode.  You can use "TP" 
to  jump to any room,  and "GET ALL" followed by an object number to  get  that 
object.

 Pools of Radiance - SSI

 This  trick might not only work on "Pools" but also on all other  AD&D  games. 
During  an attack,  wait until your opponents attacks and then press  ALTERNATE 
and X. The message "The Gods Intervene" will appear and you'll win.
