 Ms. Pacman

 Need more lives? You can swipe five of the devils by pressing PAUSE, OPTION 1, 
B,  B,  A,  A,  OPTION 1, UNPAUSE. If, on the other hand, you fancy the idea of 
commandeering a superfast Ms.  Pacman,  try this: PAUSE, OPTION 1, A, OPTION 1, 
UNPAUSE.
