        ORGINAL GERMAN TEXT AT THE END OF THIS ARTICLE

Eclipse Software is at the moment the only German Software-firm that
develops software for the Atari Falcon.
Eclipse Software has made name on the ST(E) with games like "Wings of Death"
"Lethal Xcess", "Monster Business", "Stone Age" and "Last Ninja Remix".
There are no new games released recently by Eclipse and the release dates
of the Falcon games are constantly delayed.
Well, I wanted to know what was going on, so I made a short phonecall to
Eclipse to get a clearer view on things.


ML:   Hello, I want to ask you when the new games for the Falcon would be
      released.

MARC: That's not 100% shure yet.
      We need around 4-6 months or even more, this means that you don't
      have to expect anything till the middle of 1994.

ML:   Maybe you don't want to release the games because the Falcon only
      sold around 15000 (in Europe)units.

MARC: Bull, If the games where ready, we would have released them immediatly 
      on the market, but we have lost much time negotiating with Atari.
      We also have to work on the gameplay and improve it.
      When we release the game now, we probably have a technical good game
      but with bad gameplay.
      It's also very likely that we have to develop a PC-version in order
      to finance the game and get the production cost's out of it.

ML:   All in all, there are two games at the moment for the Falcon and they
      are both worthless.
      Where are the games that Atari has promised us on the Cebit'93 !?

MARC: The lack of games would probably be declared by the Jaguar.
      In the beginning a lot of firms were busy with the development of
      Falcon games and then came Atari that told them to start developing
      games for the Jaguar first.
      After the Jaguar games they could continue their work for the Falcon.
      But I'm certain that the Falcon will profit from the Jaguar.

ML:   Why ?

MARC: Atari has returned on the console market and can make a lot of money
      with the Jaguar. 
      That money can be used for the futher development and production of the
      Falcon.
      There also is a chance that some games that will be made for the Jaguar
      also will be released for the Falcon.

ML:   Is it really possible to convert games for the Falcon without loosing
      much of the orginal quality. 
      The Falcon doesn't have the much calculation power as the Jaguar and  
      not that many custom chips and special effects !!

MARC: Some games will be converted with minor losses on calculation power.

ML:   Will there be enough games released for the Jaguar or will it undergo
      the same fate as the Lynx ?

MARC: To begin with, there will be a lot of games released for it, the big
      software flow will start in the middle of 1994.

ML:   Bye, and much luck with your programming
