I ) Just know that DITHERING can well use the mathematic series;

A/ Smooth Empty-To-Full (points):
  a -like: ''''''*'''''*''''*'''*''*'*'**'***'****'*****'******
            6*5*4*3*2*1 apostrophes ; 1'2'3'4'5'6 stars
  b - or : ''''+'*'''+'*''+'*'+'*'+*'* ....
            the 'plus (*)' are dithering more the line -a-

B/ Rude Empty-To-Full (texture):
  a -like: '''''''*''''''*'''''**''''***'''****''*****'******
            inserted reversed half A/a= 71 61 52 43 34 25 16='*)
  b -then: +'+'+'+"+'+'+'*'+'+'*"+'+'*"*'+'*"*"+'*"*"*'*"*"*"
            mixing a '* * * * * * * * ...' line

C/ Around the 'Blend-Point' (Zoom to Middle):
     middle is like: '<-- '*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'*'* -->*
  a -stretched: '*..'*'*..'*'*'*..'*'*'*'*..'*'*'*'*'*..'*'*'*'*'*'*->
                1()..2()..3()..4()..5()..6() sets:('*); '..'= 2 blanks
  b - and: <-'*.....'*'*....'*'*'*...'*'*'*'*..'*'*'*'*'*.'*'*'*'*'*'*
                the blanks (.) are now by  5 4 3 2 1
