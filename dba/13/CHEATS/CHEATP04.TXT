 Judge Dredd - Virgin

 Boot the game and start as Dredd.  Then enter "BRUCKEN PLAYING HERO QUEST" and 
press RETURN.  Exit from the computer and HELP will help you to get to the  end 
of each level.

 J.U.G. - Microdeal

 Hold  down  "Escape" on the title screen and click the mouse  pointer  on  the 
right edge of the screen. You'll get infy eny and nine livy.

and "SIRIUS".

 Karate Kid II - Anco

 "P" skips a level.  Sounds like a Steve Bak game to me. According to some, you 
have  to  enter your name as "MYAGI" in the hiscore table in order  to  achieve 
this in the first place. This might not be necessary at all, though.

 Kick Off 2 - Anco

 Press all function keys in order F1,  F2,  F3, F4....etc. Do this twice during 
play.  The words "S12" or "S14" should appear in the top right-hand corner. The 
computer goal keeper is now inactive.  When taking a penalty,  press "R" to see 
where to ball goes.

 Kid Gloves

 Pause  the game and enter "RHIANNON".  Press F9 to  become  invulnerable.  The 
other functions keys also do nice things.

 Killing Game Show - Psygnosis

 The  HELP key can be pressed during the "Good Luck" screen.  This will show  a 
map of the level.  When pressed during the "Press Fire to Continue" screen, you 
will get all your lives back!

to  jump to any room,  and "GET ALL" followed by an object number to  get  that 
object.

 Larry (Leisure Suit Larry in the Land of the Lounge Lizards) - Sierra-on-Line

 Press ALTERNATE and "X" at the title pic, followed by RETURN. No answers to be 
concocted to difficult questions then!
 Pressing [ALTERNATE] and "D" will get you into special mode.  You can use "TP" 
to  jump to any room,  and "GET ALL" followed by an object number to  get  that 
object.

 Last Duel - U.S. Gold

 Start  the ganme,  pause (F9) and hold down HELP,  LEFT SHIFT  and  "1".  Then 
unpause.  The functions keys will now move you through the levels,  and F8 will 
give you five lives.

 Last Ninja - System 3

 The level codes are the following:  1 - "SUSS",  2 - "IMED",  3 - "URTI",  4 - 
"BASD",  5  -  "NUOS" and 6 - "REOO".  These words typed together  and  spelled 
backwards go "Ooer sounds a bit rude missus".
 The codes might be for "Last Ninja 3" actually.

 Leander - Psygnosis

 Passwords: Level 2 "ZXFT", level 3 "LVFT".
 You  can  also  enter the password as "LTUS",  after  which  you  should  have 
infinite lives. Then pause with F8, skip levels with F6 and fire simultaneously 
(?!).

 Leatherneck - Microdeal

 Type  "CUTHBERTNECK"  during  the  game,  followed by  F3.  You  will  now  be 
invincible.

 Led Storm - U.S. Gold

 Enter "DAVID BROADHURST WANTS TO CHEAT" for infinite lives.
