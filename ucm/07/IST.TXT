I think you have  always heard something about  this band, because this  is the
10th album of them. If you don't have heard from them anything we have the same
problem as in the De/Vision-review... What a style of music performing And One?
Ok, it sounds like a mixture  of Front 242 and Depeche Mode again and also with
a little Techno-influence.
The songs on this piece  of work are very  different. Beside some  instrumental
things you can hear ballads and also very hard sounds, mostly with sinful text!
If you haven't heard something  from them up to now you  should listen at first
to this album at your music-shop, because the tunes are  sometimes very strange
and hardly digestible but after  two or three rounds  in your cd-player you can
find the way to this music...
