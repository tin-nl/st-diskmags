Thema Cheat-Mode:
Den haben wir durch Zufall erntdeckt. Ein Freund von mir hat beim Eingeben sei-
nes Namens  die CAPS-LOCK-Taste  benutzt. Als ich dann drann war und wie gewoht
mit "c" meine  Truppen zuordnen wollte, entbl”žte  sich mir  die gesamte  Karte

mal aktiviert, kann man mit SHIFT-M seine Moneten auf 10000 setzen. Mit SHIFT-I
gibts Infos ber die Speicherverteilung des Systems (fr Programmierer).

Thema Debug-Mode:
Mit SHIFT-D erscheint  die Meldung DEBUG, gefolgt von einer Zahl. Debug-Level 2
ist von daher interessant, weil man mit Doppelclick-Rechts dann alle Infos ber
ein Feld bekommt (Congregation, Sacrifices, Dead, Edelsteine, ...).
Den sollte man aber vor dem Beenden der Runde wieder abschalten, da er auch de-
tailierte Infos ber die Zge der Computergegner gibt (das dauert ....).

----------------------------

cheat-modes

In  the  old  version (1/94) you  have to  enter "BOSSKEY" in the  titlepicture
(Note: German Keyboard!!!) and after  that two  numbers from 1-10 (not from the
numeric pad!). The first  number sets  the amount  of bombs you  can lay at the

number sets  the amount of  bombs you can kick at the beginning. The rest works
like in the older version. I hope you have no problems to use the cheats.

SHEER AGONY (Logitron)
-----------
ATARI ST/E - FALCON

This should be no solution for  the game. At first I want to write one but I've
lost the motivation to do this. So here are  some hints and tips and so you can
save many time while playing the game...
At the following text I'll tell you where you can find usefull things...

-
At the graveyard you can get a key under the deathstone left over the stone in
front of you.
At the little bush besides the deathstone in front of you you can find a knife.
-
At the lake you can find a stick. There is the entry to a cave but you'll need
a candle and a lighter...
-
In the ground floor...
In the  right room is a bookboard  with 2 books about the house and magic. Read
them... On the  little chest of drawers on the right side  is standing a figure
without a head. You must get this head to open the chest.
In the kitchen (after the bibliothek and  the dining room) you can find several
things  like spoons, a  cup, a coffe mill, a  teapot, wine, a lighter... In the
oven you can find the key for the closed door in the first floor.
In the left room you can find tabac and  you can go to the living room. In this
room you can find  several books (besides others  also a map of  the house) and
a candle (!).
-
At the first floor...
The  right room  is a sleeping  room. After this  follows Phils sleeping  room.
Behind  the picture at the wand is a safe  which can be opened with  the little
key from the graveyard. In the safe you'll find the mixture for the antidote.
In the bathroom, left  at the wand is a secret with the code for the electronic
safe in the third floor... The code is 6825...
-
At the second floor...
In the  left room  is a board with other  books. In the  big vase is one of the
needed  medaillons. In the  room after this is another board where you can find
a kruzifix and a another book about magic.
In the right room you can find several things like a cardgame.
In the room at the left side, which was  closed, you  can find  a picture which
shows how you must put down the four medaillons at the table in the third floor
and  also the second  medaillon (over  the bed at the wand). At nearly the same
place is also a secret...
-
At the third floor...
In the middle of the screen, behind the wand is a electronic safe. In this safe
you will find the "vase of mooz".
-
In the basement...
In the cupboard in the  big room at the basement you can find motoroil and also
a fork, a knife and a lighter.
Behind the right  door in the  basement there  is a cave. This cave take you to
the waterfall at the little lake behind the house.
