                        BLACK PARTY & CONCERT GUIDE 1/2oo1
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

You'll surely  wonder about that article and you'll surely remember  that series
back in  the late nineties. The reason to spread  concert dates  for electronic/
industrial/goth bands again via UNDERCOVER is quite simple.

As you should know we removed those lists  because PHOENIX of VECTRONIX invented
a special internet page for those dates. That page was growing fast  and several
other Atarians helped out there with translating the contents and ideas. Finally
WWW.SCARECROW.NU was launched, offering the users hundreds of concert dates over
whole Europe and even the United States.

But all  ended very fast, PHOENIX didn't  supported and developped  the page any
further. So the  interface  wasn't very  userfriendly and  the page  wasn't very
known and frequented. Later on only a hand full of freax was filling up the huge
list of dates and with the time the page was fallen asleep.

Other pages  appeared with the same content and mailinglists about concert dates
popped up. So I decided to stop my work for SCARECROW but I still want  to share
informations  about concerts and gigs of Electro/Goth acts with other interested
peoples and because of this I'm using my  own publication  again, even if I know
that I wont reach a lot of people who are interested in them, I try it anyway.

And a message  to RIKARD: heja mate, if  you think about a  resurrection  of the
page, get in touch with me, I would help also a second time!

Ok, enough for this, let's re-invent an old UCM series again :)
