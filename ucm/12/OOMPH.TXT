{*f1OOMPH! - "UNREIN"{*f0

produced by Virgin Records 1998

FINALLY  here it is, the  latest album  of the  german  industrial terrorists
Oomph! ... and their fifth  album is a real  mess! And it's the first release
under the major label VIRGIN.
But there is  surely a little problem... only a few people have heard of this
band before and it's  really time to present their work to all the people out
there. Many  guys are  impressed by  RAMMSTEIN, thinking  that  this band has
invented a fully  new style of music, but  they're so  fucking wrong, not the
hyped RAMMSTEINers have created the hard metal-electro-crossover, accompanied
with provoking lyrics... no, RAMMSTEIN are only a good but finally cheap copy
of OOMPH!  Yep, they're making this kind of  music since 1992 and the guys of
RAMMSTEIN have said that they're  influenced by them. And I think this is the
most important and a real friendly move of the RAMMSTEINers.
Anyway with the new release they're showing that they're still the leaders in
this style of music! There are  besides the above  mentioned band many groups
around, copying their style, but no one reaches the perfect harmony of brutal
sounds and updigging lyrics, without being only hard... on this album you can
find some very slow and real interesting  songs too, besides  the usual dance
floor stompers and club hits.
But before I will  talk more about the songs itself let me give you a list of
the contents...

o1. Mutters Schoû
o2. Unsere Rettung
o3. Die Maske
o4. My Hell
o5. Gekreuzigt
o6. Zero Endorphine
o7. Willst Du mein Leben entern?
o8. (Why I'll never be) clean again
o9. Unrein
1o. Anniversary
11. Foil
12. Bastard
13. Another Disease
14. Meine Wunden

horrrrr... 14 songs with about 70 minutes pure power! Alone the playlenght of
this album shows  the potential of the band. They have really  no problems to
shout out their feelings and to integrate them finally into the right musical
atmosphere. They're searching the confrontation with the listeners and trying
all to woke them up...
And so their  latest masterpiece  contains  songs  about love, belief, animal
testings and all kind of unclean things.
But when I remember some of  their old stuffs I must admit that they have not
only developped their sound much more, also the lyrics are written better and
finally the  singer DERO has  formed out  his voice in  a kind you  had never
thought, he's  shouting, screaming, whispering  and singing clear like glass,
just cool! I don't  want to  write something about every song  itself. For me
all the  lyrics are  interesting  and from  the  sound  structures  the  most
interesting  stuffs  are "Gekreuzigt" (the  usual  metal electro  crossover),
"Zero Endorphine" (an instrumental  with many  electronic  stuff), "Foil" (it
remembers a bit to some  slow Depeche Mode songs!!!), "Bastard" (remembers me
to their  album "Defekt", same  style, cool  album too!), and "Meine  Wunden"
(very electronic between except the  refrain). But I will notice that all the
other songs have  their nice moments too and for a real Oomph!-fan this album
is a real MUST! But also people who are interested in  this sound, especially
all the RAMMSTEIN-freax  should check it out, to find out something about the
new sound of the nineties, which wasn't created by their idols...
Finally here are coming all the lyrics, hope the  english people are open for
the style to  release german  and english  lyrics together on an  album, it's
some kind of an Oomph!-brandsign...
