          "SPOT" by AND ONE{*f0

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

released by : Dark Wings / Machinery Records / Germany
price       : 33 DM

This is  another great album of  the german industrial/electro-band AND ONE and
it's one of the best I think. You can hear great stomping rhythms and above all
very good texts, even if  some of the  songs are a  bit strange, like the crazy
sex-tale SPONTANVERKEHR.
What you can find at this album?

01 - Wild Pain
02 - Life Isn't Easy In Germany
03 - Consequence Of Time
04 - Spontanverkehr
05 - Friend Of Stars
06 - Hall Of Souls
07 - Recover You
08 - Der Erste Stein
09 - Tanz Der Arroganz
10 - The And
11 - Spot
12 - Wild Pain Mix (bonus-track)

So, I wouldn't write  more shit here, just  go to the next record-shop  and buy
this great  album. If you're a friend of hard electro-music which  sounds a bit
like a mix of Depeche Mode / Front 242 and other  stars on the Synthie/Electro-
scene you must not test the album before buying...

And finally all the song-texts...
