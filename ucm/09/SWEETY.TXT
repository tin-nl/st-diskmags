-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                    AND ONE
                                "SWEETY SWEETY"
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

maxi/EP, released by Virgin 1997

Here comes the  second maxi from the  german electronic  rulers. This time they
have pressed the dullest song from their new album "NORDHAUSEN" to a single.
Now they have not only bonus-tracks, but also 3 different versions of the title
track. But in spite of it you  will find 3 new  tracks and a  hidden mix of the
song "HIGH" which was already included on the "SOMETIMES"-EP.
Ok, now for the contents:

01. Sweety Sweety / Radio Edit
02. Ich Esse Ein Brot
03. Das Autofahrerlied
04. Zaresta Imano
05. Sweety Sweety / Abdul Tanz Mix
06. Sweety Sweety / Instrumental
07. High / Remix - Hidden Track

Hum, ok, the title-track is very funny and without any deeper meaning, only fun
and party-music, no question. But I don't like the two other versions very much
and I think it would be better not to mix up the songs only for profit.
Hum, the second song is shit, at least the text! Don't  know why they have such
woman-hostile  lyrics  included, it's only primitive and  even bad  experiences
with a woman don't gave the right to write such a bullshit...
The third and fourth  songs are ok, but nothing special. The hidden track is in
comparison with the other new songs the best on this CD, at least for me...
