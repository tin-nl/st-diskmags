-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                     QNTAL
                                   "QNTAL 2"
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

released by Gymnastic Records 1995

This is the second  fulltime-album of  QNTAL, a german  dark wave / electronic-
band. You will find a  sort of medieval  songs in a  new sound-dress on this CD
with texts in latin, middle high german and old french.

The following tracks are included:

01. Introitus
02. Palestinalied
03. Frhling
04. Hymni Nocturnales
05. Vos Attestor
06. Herbst
07. Abaelard
08. Virgo Splendens
09. Trobar Clus
10. Sine Nomine
11. Ab Vox d'Angel
12. Epilog

Besides some very  melodic stuff (like the club-hit "Palestinalied" or the very
nice "Frhling") you can hear also  a bit strange  sounds and  the one or other
very melancholic song ("Herbst").
The most interesting things are the original lyrics from the  Middle Ages. This
time no one  can say that  the whole dark wave  and  new romantic scene is only
tired  of the life  and all thinking  on the death. The  lyrics were written by
such great poets like Walther von der Vogelweide or Konrad von Wrzburg.
The most songs  living  from the  great  voice of  Sigrid and  the more or less
electronic sound. So  you can  dance to some  of the them  or just sit back and
just listen to them.
If you go  to a record-shop hear  at first to this album, it isn't suitable for
all  people. The best songs in  my eyes and ears  are: Palestinalied, Frhling,
Herbst, Abaelard, Trobar Clus and Ab Vox d'Angel. The others  are also  for  me
very hard to digest...

Last but not least the lyrics...

At first  the original texts and the german translation, english follows at the
end...

------------------------------

Only now my life gains a more profunding meaning

the holy earth and the

I arrived at the city

Many countries beautiful and rich and
splendid, I have seen:
you outshine them all.
how many wonderful things have happened here!
was it not a great miracle
that a virgin gave birth to a child
more sacred than all hosts of angels?

Here the Pure one allowed himself to be baptized
and made the people pure.
master became servant
and servants were set free.
otherwise we would have been lost;
thanks to spear, cross and thorns.
woe be to you, heathens, that you are angry about it.

Out of the grave where he lay down
the son went into hell.
his father was always with him
as was the spirit which nobody
can separate from the son and the father:
all are one, smooth and straight like an arrow,
as the triune God which appeared to Abraham.

In this land, so he prophesied,
there will be a dreadful doomsday.
then the widow will be revenged
and the wise and the poor may
complain of the harm
that people inflicted upon them.
