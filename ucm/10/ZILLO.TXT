-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
                                VARIOUS ARTISTS
                             - ZILLO CLUB-HITS 2 -
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

sampler, published by Zillo Magazine 1997

This is one  of the latest  Zillo-samplers and features  again a  cross-section
through  the whole world of dark-wave and  gothic music. I don't know if I will
buy again a sampler of the Zillo-team  because after their festival this summer
I've decided to distance me from them. Their leading saying "From the scene for
the scene" are only empty words and not worth  any minute to think about. But I
have bought this  sampler some weeks before  my decision and will review it for
you this time...

This time  I will give  you some  little information  about every  song. Let us
start with the one by THE MERRY THOUGHTS. For all which  don't know this band I
should mention that  these guys are  regarded as the  reincarnation of the good
old  SISTERS OF MERCY, and this  not in a  bad kind! They're  making  powerfull
gothic-rock  and even with  the first sounds  you're remembered  to their great
idols.
The next  two songs  are slower  gothic and  not bad. The  track by  THEATRE OF
TRAGEDY is  the darkest  on this  sampler. For  a norwegian  band it is  a  bit
strange to use  german lyrics, but it sounds great. You can listen to powerfull
gothic-metal with very high female and very deep male voices! Cool!
After the  gothic-track  of MOONSPELL which I  don't like very much the sampler
changes  the music-style and  with COVENANT  and SUICIDE COMMANDO are real good
Electro/EBM-heroes included and you should know at least the great track by the
second one...
The next track is a bit strange... it's a nice  electro-pop-song with very good
lyrics about the intolerant black scene and only the guys which can laugh about
these lyrics have the right  point of view  in the black scene... But many guys
will find their own person in these lines and they will loose their mask...
The next one is wellknown and should be played also in all those village-discos
but it's a nice and trashy electro-pop-song.
The next one is pure synthie-pop and not bad. One of the best songs is the next
one. It's the  new band of the Strange Ways label (De/Vision, Wolfsheim...) and
also synth-pop, very nice. It remembers to Wolfsheim and features nice melodies
and the right pinch of melancholy... After this song I'm very interested in the
debut-album called "Mentality".
The PANKOW-song  is very hard  electro and not my  thing... also  the next one,
which is more goth-stuff isn't the right stuff.
One of the most interesting songs  is the last one by KIRLIAN CAMERA! Very nice
and slow melodies with a female voice... Great stuff for laying back and relax!
It is taken from the album "Pictures from eternity" and  also this album brings
some nice and mysterious songs to your ears...
Conclusion: all in one it's a nice sampler with nearly all styles of dark music
and especially for all those people which will take a look over the edge of the
plate it's very recommendable!
